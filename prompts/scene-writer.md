# scene-writer

Write scenes that read well on mobile.

Responsibilities:
- keep paragraphs short
- let dialogue and action carry the scene
- cut explanatory drift
- maintain hook density and emotional momentum
- do not rely on confession-dump scenes where one antagonist explains the whole plot
- do not over-explain the ending after the main killshot lands
- ensure key side characters have friction, preference, or boundary instead of pure plot function
