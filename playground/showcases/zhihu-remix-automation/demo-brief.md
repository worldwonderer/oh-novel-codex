# Demo brief: Zhihu remix automation showcase

Keep the rewritten story recognizably close to the original narrative machine while making it feel more publishable.

Priorities:

1. preserve the original emotional engine
2. keep the core relationship conflict legible
3. increase hook density and chapter-end pull
4. strengthen the ending strike without changing the story into a different genre
5. avoid wild skeleton replacement just to prove originality

The ideal outcome is:

- same story lane
- stronger readability
- better momentum
- better review handoff
